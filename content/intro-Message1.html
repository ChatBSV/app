1. Commands

Commands

Chat:

[ENTER] - Submit your prompt.

[SHIFT+ENTER] - Skip a line.

Enter Key Control:

/ENTER ON - Enable Enter key for submission.

/ENTER OFF - Disable Enter key for submission.

Generating Content:

/MEME {Prompt} - Generate memes.

/IMAGINE {Prompt} --format: - Generate images.

Setting the Model:

Default Models: GPT 3.5 & DALL-E 3.

/GPT 3 - Set GPT-3.5 Turbo model.

/GPT 4 - Set GPT-4 model.

/DALL-E 2 - Set DALL-E 2 model.

/DALL-E 3 - Set DALL-E 3 model.

Get Help:

/Help - Get information.



2. How It Works

How it Works

Connect:

Click the Connect button to log in with your Handcash wallet or create a new one.

Chat:

Type your prompt and hit the [ENTER] key or click the Send button to submit.

Use [SHIFT+ENTER] to skip a line.

Using Commands:

To switch between models, use the model name and number starting with /gpt or /dalle.

/GPT 3 - Sets GPT-3.5 Turbo as the model.

/GPT 4 - Sets GPT-4 as the model.


Example:

Switch to GPT 4 and continue the conversation

/GPT 4
/DALLE 2 - Sets DALL-E 2 as the model.

/DALLE 3 - Sets DALL-E 3 as the model.


Example:

Switch to DALLE 2 before generating an image

/DALLE 2

Generating Media

Use the /imagine and /meme commands to generate content:

/IMAGINE - Generate images using AI.


Example:

Generate an image with your preferred model

/IMAGINE a futuristic city.
/MEME - Create a unique AI-generated meme.


Example:

Generate a new meme

/MEME deez cat got memez

Image Formatting

You can further customize your outputs with optional formatting parameters:

DALLE 2

/imagine {prompt}

--format 512

(Dimensions valid for DALL-E 2)


Example:

Format images on DALL-E 2

/imagine a futuristic city --format 512
DALLE 3

/imagine {prompt}

--format horizontal

(Horizontal and vertical formats are valid for DALL-E 3)


Example:

Format images on DALL-E 3

/imagine a futuristic city --format vertical

5. Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

  • Your Chats Are Private: We uphold the highest standards of privacy. Your conversations are kept confidential and are not shared with any third parties other than the AI service provider directly.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal data or information from your chats. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us.
  • Data Submission to AI: To provide you with accurate and meaningful responses, we securely submit your queries to the AI model selected.
  • User-Friendly Display: The responses generated by AI are displayed to you in a user-friendly format for your convenience.
  • Local Storage: For your convenience and reference, we save the chat history in your local storage. This allows you to revisit and continue your conversations seamlessly. You may delete your history at any time.
  • No Access, No Records: We do not have access to your chats beyond providing you with the service. We do not keep any records of your conversations.
  • Security Measures: To ensure the safety and security of our community, users are identified by their HandCash wallets. This helps us address any potential misuse or criminal activity.
